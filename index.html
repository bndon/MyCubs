Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our daycare services

You can book care through our mobile app or website up to 24 hours in advance. Cancellations made at least 12 hours before the scheduled time receive a full refund. For last-minute cancellations, a 50% fee applies.

We maintain strict safety standards including background checks for all staff, secure entry systems, regular sanitization, child-proofed facilities, and CCTV monitoring. All staff are trained in first aid and CPR.

Drop-off and pick-up times are flexible within our operating hours (7 AM - 7 PM). Parents must sign children in and out using our digital system. Only authorized individuals listed in your account can pick up your child.

Our app allows you to book care, make payments, view real-time updates and photos, access CCTV feeds, communicate with caregivers, view daily reports, and manage your account details.

Yes, we provide nutritious meals and snacks appropriate for your child's age. We accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies. Our menu is designed by a nutritionist and prepared fresh daily.

We maintain a 1:3 ratio for infants (0-12 months), 1:4 for toddlers (1-2 years), and 1:6 for preschoolers (3-5 years). This ensures each child receives proper attention and care.
